WooBooster Analytics gives store owners a focused view of orders, revenue, courier performance, and fraud trends.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard highlights daily revenue, successful deliveries, returns, and pending follow-ups. It is designed for quick decisions during active campaigns.

## Available Metrics

| Metric | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue | Track campaign and daily sales performance |
| Delivered Orders | Measure fulfillment success |
| Return Rate | Spot courier or product issues |
| Fraud Blocks | Review prevented dispatch risk |

## Exporting Data

Export reports for reconciliation, team review, or campaign analysis. Keep exports private because they may include customer and order information.

## Reading Trends

Look for sudden increases in returns, stuck shipped orders, or low purchase tracking quality. These signals often reveal operational issues before they become expensive.
