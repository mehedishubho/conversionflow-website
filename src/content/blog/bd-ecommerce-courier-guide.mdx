Courier operations are where many Bangladeshi WooCommerce stores lose time. The best courier choice depends on coverage, speed, return handling, and how your team communicates with customers after checkout.

## Courier Comparison

| Courier | Coverage | Speed | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Steadfast | Strong nationwide reach | Consistent in major cities | High-volume fashion and electronics |
| Pathao | Strong urban coverage | Fast city delivery | Dhaka-first stores and campaigns |
| RedX | Broad ecommerce footprint | Balanced delivery and returns | Stores that need structured reporting |

## What to Automate First

### Order Submission

Manual copy-paste creates errors in phone numbers and addresses. WooBooster sends WooCommerce order details directly to the configured courier so your team can focus on confirmation calls and packing.

### Status Sync

Courier status should return to WooCommerce automatically. When delivery, return, or hold status changes, your support team sees the update without opening another dashboard.

### Return Review

Returned parcels are expensive. Use Fraud Shield with courier history to flag repeat return behavior before dispatching future COD orders.

## Choosing a Workflow

Start with the courier your team already trusts, then connect additional providers when you need better district coverage or campaign-specific routing. WooBooster is designed to keep these workflows consistent even when you use more than one courier partner.
